Keyword gap analysis should be a standard section in every audit dashboard (101 Drogéria, Bombazlava.sk, etc.).

**Why:** The "aha moment" for clients is seeing specific product keywords with high volume where they're invisible but competitors rank. This is what sells the audit at a beer meeting.

**How to apply:**
- Use Ahrefs to find high-volume product keywords where competitor ranks but client doesn't
- Cross-reference with whether client has ads running on those keywords (Ads? column)
- Verify positions in live Google SERP (don't trust Ahrefs positions blindly)
- Name the section in human/business language: "Príležitosti pre produkty, ktoré trendujú" not "Keyword Gap Analýza"
- Always check Google Reviews (Maps) for the client — it's part of the 12-step methodology
- Always check Heureka reviews
- Show the gap visually: volume number badge + competitor position vs client position
