---
name: Verify ads by checking landing pages
description: When auditing ads, always visit the landing page/website to verify claims before writing findings
type: feedback
---

When auditing ads (Meta Ad Library, Google Transparency Center), always visit the actual landing page the ad links to before making claims about relevance or waste.

**Why:** Hiring ads for Aquapond were labeled as "forgotten / wasted spend" but the landing page showed positions were still actively open. This would have destroyed credibility on a sales call.

**How to apply:**
- For every ad that seems problematic, click through to the destination URL
- Check if the content is still live and relevant
- Don't assume ads are "forgotten" just because they've been running long — verify on the website
- Adjust severity and language based on what the landing page actually shows
- Also applies to: product ads (is the product still available?), promo ads (is the promo still valid?), seasonal ads (is it still the right season?)
- Meta Ad Library compresses video/image quality — don't call out visual quality based on Ad Library previews alone
