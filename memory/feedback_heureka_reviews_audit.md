When auditing a Slovak eshop, always visit their Heureka profile (obchody.heureka.sk) and read customer reviews as a standard part of the audit.

**Why:** Good reviews are an untapped gold mine — they can be used in ad copy, on-site social proof, creatives, and Google Seller Ratings. Most eshops don't leverage this. It's a quick win recommendation that shows real value.

**How to apply:**
- Find the Heureka shop profile (URL format: obchody.heureka.sk/{shop-slug}/recenze/)
- Note: overall rating, number of reviews, % recommends, avg delivery time
- Pull out the best 5-6 quotes with customer names
- Tag key themes from reviews (speed, service, quality, expertise, etc.)
- Recommend: (1) use in ad copy, (2) add social proof to website, (3) create testimonial-based creatives, (4) enable Google Seller Ratings
- This is part of a broader "eshop performance improvement" audit standard
